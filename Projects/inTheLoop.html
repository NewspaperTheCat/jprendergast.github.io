-= In The Loop = Itch.io Page =-

 This was my and one other's submission to the GMTK2025 Game Jam. Taking the theme of "Loop" in a unique direction, we went with spreading a secret around an office party, and what screams office party more than golf? Gameplay features golf controls in a bustling environment that must be conquered to deliver your message.

This distinctive idea and execution netted us #62 in Creativity, placing us in the top 0.6% percentile across 9,583 total submissions.

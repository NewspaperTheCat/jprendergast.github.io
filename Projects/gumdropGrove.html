-= Gumdrop Grove = Itch.io Page =-

 Gumdrop Grove began as a submission to WPI IGDA's New Tech Game Jam 2024. As such, it was my first introduction to Godot. Despite a peculiar start, I have since brought it to game expos and ported it onto an arcade cabinet.

 Venture into the words in hopes to find your lost dog, traversing through a roguelike dungeon crawler format. The game features an ever-growing candlyland labyrinth and tests the player's navigation back out of the grove each night.

 Grumdrop Grove is continuing to receive updates.

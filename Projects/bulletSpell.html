-= Bullet Spell = Itch.io Page =-

 Bullet Spell was my submission to the Ludum Dare 54 game jam. As it was a COMPO submission, all assets were made by me within the strict 48-hour timeframe. I went on a bit after the jam developing the game to include major feedback and a tutorial.

 The game is a hectic bullet hell that is procedurally crafty by a dynamic collage of 5 unique enemies arranged by an elaborate wave spawning directory inspired by Risk of Rain 2.

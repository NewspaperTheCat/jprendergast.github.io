-= And the World Went Dark = Steam Page =-

 This project was the Major Qualifying Project of WPI seniors during my first year there. I reached out and secured a position on the term as an Independent Study Project. The game was run via various sub-departments and abiding by very formal game development procedures.

 The game features a in depth lore and a core gameplay loop most similar to games like Slay the Spire. In place of a deck, the player managed equipment across four party members to discover explore the vast breadth of available items and discover never before seen synergies.

-= Contributions =-

 My main contributions came from the implementations of pre-design items and the associated artifact & consumable management systems. During my time working on the project I implemented 38/45 of the artifacts and all consumables.

 Ahead of schedule on the elements I was placed on, I was moved to integrating the shop scene: provided the design and layout, I implemented shop navigation, spawning logic, and integration with the inventory and save data systems.

 I was brought back on during the start of the following summer to implemented more items and otherwise polish certain data saving systems.
